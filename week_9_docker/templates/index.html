Free! Anime

About Free!

Free! is a Japanese anime television series produced by Kyoto Animation and Animation Do. The story is centered around members of a high school swimming team. The first season, Free! - Iwatobi Swim Club, premiered in July 2013, and since then, it has gained a massive following worldwide.
